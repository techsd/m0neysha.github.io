About Me
Hello! I'm a junior at University of California, Berkeley pursuing degrees in Computer Science & Cognitive Science. I'm interested in online education platforms and bringing computer science and technology into urban areas.

I migrated to Hayward, California in 2001 from the Fiji Islands and have been in the Bay ever since. In high school, I spent my time volunteering as a tutor at the local library and working as a tutor for Kumon, an after-school learning program. These experiences have solidified by passion for working in education.

In Spring 2013, I took my first programming course CS10, the Beauty and Joy of Computing, and loved it. At Cal, I'm taking Computer Architecture (CS61C) and a Ruby on Rails course. Recently, I became an instructor for a Girls Who Code club in the bay area where I host weekly club sessions to expose girls in underserved areas to a comprehensive and unique CS education.

In my free time, I love getting lost on soundcloud and youtube and finding new artists and songs to add to my studying/coding/etc playlists. I've been a runner since 2008 and try to squeeze in some 5ks here and there.

I'm looking to develop skills and learn as much as I can about computer science. The best way to reach me is email.
