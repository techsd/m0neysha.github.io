MUSIC
Welcome to my music corner of the internet. I grew up with a groovy father, an avid cd collector, who played 70's and 80's funk, smooth jazz, blues, reggae and pop developing my old school taste. Artists and bands like Michael Jackson, Prince, Madonna, Commodores, Kool & the Gang, The Gap Band, The Whispers, Bob Marley, UB40, Inner Circle, & Marcus Miller were some of my favorites.
