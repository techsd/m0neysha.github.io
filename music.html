Music
Welcome to my music corner of the internet. I grew up with a groovy father, an avid cd collector, who played 70's and 80's funk, smooth jazz, blues, reggae and pop. Artists and bands like Michael Jackson, Prince, Madonna, Commodores, Kool & the Gang, The Gap Band, The Whispers, Bob Marley, UB40, Inner Circle, & Marcus Miller were some of my favorites.
